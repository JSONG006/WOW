NTU World Of Wisdom

The World of Wisdom (WOW) is an initiative by the TEDxNTU team that aspires to serve as a think tank that attracts the brightest minds and creative spirits in Nanyang Technological University and Singapore. Through its activities, WOW seeks to foster a community of thinkers, engineers, scientists and artists with a healthy blend of idealism, pragmatism and a disregard for the impossible.
